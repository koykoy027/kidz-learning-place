Our Education Program

In our curriculum, we embrace Dewey's philosophy by providing children with ample opportunities for hands-on exploration and discovery. We believe that learning is most effective when it is meaningful and rooted in real-world experiences. By immersing children in activities that allow them to interact with their surroundings, we promote deeper understanding and critical thinking skills. Progressive approach serves as a valuable cornerstone of our educational philosophy, guiding us in creating a dynamic learning environment that nurtures curiosity, creativity, Christian values, and lifelong learning skills in young children. And our holistic approach ensures that every student has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential. We believe that by nurturing the whole child, we can empower them to become confident, well-rounded individuals who are prepared to succeed in all aspects of life.
